    Capability Pillars and Concepts

    This section provides a business overview of Capability Pillars that constitute MonE platform and highlights the critical concepts that differentiate MonE. for a further detailed description of features, please refer to sections Functional Features Overview and Nonfunctional Features Overview.

    The following diagram depicts the pillars of MonE considering different viewpoints, such as Key Aspects and Unique Digital Experience, Operational Experience and Technology stack.

    The following sections provide details related to each viewpoint.

    1. Next-Gen Monetization Concepts and Features

    1.1. MonE Monetization Template Models

    Definition

    Monetization refers to standards and processes defined/adopted for deriving revenue from the value you offer to your customers.

    Within an ecosystem, as part of a digital platform based business, a service operator will have many different service offerings. Different service offerings will have their own pricing strategies for different price types (onetime, recurrent, usage etc.) to address different value propositions to the customer. Moreover, pricing and tariff determination must also take into account the values of additional qualifiers such as timestamp, volume, duration, location, channel, cost introduced by involved business partners, infrastructure etc.

    MonE Monetization Template Models provide support for the next generation digital business monetization options amid the complex ecosystem of service providers and other involved business partners as the following:

    • Define and execute the strategy for B2C (end customer) with flexible rules for determining involved customers, who pays for what, when, and which charges, how charges are calculated from charging metrics and which tariffs to used that derive pricing methods subject to calculations

    • Define and execute the strategy for B2B (partners) with flexible rules for determining involved partners, revenue sharing rules, who pays or needs to be settled for what, when, and which charges, how charges are calculated from charging metrics contexts and which tariffs or end customer charges to used that derive pricing methods subject to calculations

    1.2. MonE Tariffs

    Definition

    Tariffs denote an integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used by Charging and Billing processes with built-in conditional rulesets contained.

    MonE Platform utilizes two types of tariffs:

    • Charging Tariff (a.k.a Charging Price Plan): An integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used by Charging processes used for Price calculation of a sales items.
    • Billing Tariff (a.k.a Billing Price Plan): An integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used by Billing processes for aggregation/classification/collation of financial artifacts, such as; bill line items, GL allocation items, tax items, cost center allocation items etc.

    1.3. Flexible Discount and Promo Models

    In addition to next-gen configurable capabilities summarized in Section 1.1 that allows reflecting enterprise strategies onto charging and billing, MonE provides multiple ways of discounting and promotional capabilities.

    Definition

    Discount refers to a reduction/value alteration made on Price that impacts (depending on application time) the Charge and/or bill line item amount (bill line items constitute the bill or settlement statement).

    Promotion refers to an integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used to increase sales, product public awareness. Promotions shall not be confused with Discounts. A promotion may use one or more several tools to increase sales and awareness, such as; loyalty points, bonus points, advertising and discounting.

    Thus considering the two main sub domains of MonE being Charging and Billing, out-of-the-box provided discounting and promotional capabilities are as the following:

    Application Time Type Name Description
    Charging Discount Price Alteration An alteration as a fixed amount or percentage on the price. Typically applied for non-usage related charging on one-time and/or recurrent charges related to orders submitted to MonE.
    Charging Discount Free Units Volume or Monetary based (on defined periods auto reset) quotas that allow free of charge usages.
    Charging Discount Tiered/Progressive Discounts Volume or Monetary driven one or multiple thresholds based modification/reductions on original charge amount that encourages consumption of a related service or product offering.
    Charging Discount Cross Discounts Volume or Monetary driven one or multiple thresholds that allows multi-levels of discounting on service offering A, when aggregated usage/purchase of service offering B reaches a certain threshold ranges.
    Billing Bill Line Item Discounts Bill Line Item based Tiered/Progressive Discounts Volume or Monetary driven one or multiple thresholds that allows multi-levels of discounting on lump sum amount on an bill line item prior to generation of official financial invoice, such as extra 10% discount on invoice amount of offering A, if the usage amount after Charging discounts has exceeded 50$ in the current bill cycle
    Billing Invoice Discounts Invoice Amount Discounts A reduction in lump sum amount of the invoice irrelevant of the items that constitute the total invoice charge.

    1.4. B2C and B2B Convergence

    As illustrated in Section 1.1, MonE allows a consolidated event driven platform to manage B2C and B2B related charging and billing with the required support for multiple pricing types, charging and billing modes, such as apply usage charge on party-B using the tariff of party-A with a billing mode of in-advance.

    1.5. Multiple Operation Modes

    MonE provides several plug and play operational modes considering the very different nature of charging and billing domains.

    • Charging: MonE provides the mechanisms and interfaces that allows file based offline charging, event based on-line charging and session based online charging that utilize the same rating and balance management functionality resulting in charged transactions fed into Billing domain

    • Billing: MonE provides the mechanisms and interfaces that allows for an individual account immediate generation of hot bills items, on-demand bills, quotation bills. MonE also supports Batch Bill generation that allows mass generation of consolidated bills for multiple subscriptions that pertain a specific bill cycle term and a market segmentation.

    1.6. Decoupled and Integrated Open APIs

    MonE comes out of the box with support for the industry leading and widely accepted TMFORUM Open APIs that relate to billing, such as Account Management or ProductOrder APIs. This approach allows not only minimizing the time-to-deploy, but also drastically reduces integration costs of the enterprise organizations that operate on standards compliant business application components, such as Product Catalogue Management and/or Order Management systems.

    2. Operational Excellence

    Cloud native and microservice architectures gain a lot of popularity due to the ever-increasing complexity of systems. Thus, Communication service providers (CSPs) and suppliers are committed to delivering cloud-native, reusable software components, and replacing legacy OSS/BSS to enable industry to transform business agility and unlock growth.

    In this regards, once considered as an second degree of importance factor, Operational Excellence now becomes as critical or even more critical than ever. CSPs once focusing on edge case functionalities now prefer a system with enough functional capabilities, but that can scale and comes out-of-the-box with distributed analytical capabilities.

    3. Standard Best Of Breed Technology Stack

    MonE platform targets open source software usage in order to minimize license and other commercials costs. Cloud native and microservice architectures gain a lot of popularity due to the ever-increasing complexity of systems with many alike tools for the vendors to build their products. However, utilizing the right technology for the right purpose is crucial. For further details, please refer to MonE Cloud Native Technology Landscape.

    4. Integration and Open APIs

    Open Digital Architecture initiative, led by TMFORUM focuses on the principles, guidelines for delivering cloud-native, reusable software components, and replacing legacy OSS/BSS to enable industry to transform business agility and unlock growth. Due to the ever increasing complexity of systems, it becomes more and more critical for a system and all its components to be ready to integrate, communicate via and against industry standard open APIs.

    MonE platform components support TMFORUM Open APIs and ready to integrate with 3PP legacy systems via conventional API managers such as WSO2. For a further detailed specification of APIs, please refer to APIs View.

    5. Intelligence and Actionable Analytics

    Considering the ever increasing complexity and distributed nature of new generation systems, the following elements are the keys to ensure uninterrupted system operation.

    • Reliable Infrastructure with built-in redundancy and no single point of failure
    • Adequate system resources to handle fluctuating processing requirements
    • Resilience & Scalability seamlessly resilient & scalable application architectures and dynamic deployment mechanisms
    • Continuous data collections, periodic processes, audit and monitoring to ensure infrastructure and application elements meet and exceed requirements
    • Diagnosis, detection and prediction of any infrastructure, application or resource issues before they impact system operation with automated actions*

    The last two are directly related to the systems capability for Intelligence and Actionable Analytics. In this regards, MonE provides built-in and out-of-the-box capabilities for operational analytics and integration to intelligence systems.
