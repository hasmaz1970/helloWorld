    Abbreviations and Definitions

    This section provides definitive explanations of used terms and abbreviations used throughout this present Online 360-Library.

    1. Terms and Definitions

    The following table provides a glossary of terms and definitions used in this library:

    Definition Description
    Billing Tariff (a.k.a Billing Price Plan) An integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used by Billing processes for aggregation/classification/collation of financial artifacts, such as; bill line items, GL allocation items, tax items, cost center allocation items etc.
    Charge The actual demanded/calculated Price (please refer to definition for Price) for a sales item; services rendered or hard/soft goods supplied, considering aspects other than the set Tariff price. Actual charge of the same thing being sold to different buyers, may be different compared to the regular Price set on a Tariff, as Charge typically will consider other price alterations, discounts and business rules applied specific to the buyer.
    Charging Tariff (a.k.a Charging Price Plan) An integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used by Charging processes used for Price calculation of a sales items.
    Customization or Customer Adaptation Refers to a customer adaptation for the purpose of integrating or adapting out-of-the-box provided product components and/or APIs. Such customization examples(including, but not limited to) are: add-on software packages as task/protocol adapters, file import/export utilities, custom specific process/workflow configurations etc.
    Configuration Indicates any configurational change to control functional and/or non-functional aspects of the system or one of its components. The change may be (including, but not limited to) via updating of information that may reside in files, relational/non-relation databases, key-value store updates over distributed configuration management service APIs, etc.
    Discount A reduction/value alteration made on Price that impacts (depending on application time) the Charge and/or billed item amount. Discounts may be:
    • Simple Discounts; 10% discount on the list price.
    • Friends/Family Discounts; served party defines/declares a limited set of destination addresses via a relevant product offering, and transaction discounted if destination address is a member of this set.
    • Community Discounts; Discount applied as part of tariff rules that check if both served and other party subject to transaction belong to a community group.
    • Free Units or Aggregation based Discounts; Free up to 4GB of usage, or 50% discount after 300 minutes of international call.
    • Complex cross-discounts; 1GB of free high-speed data connection for every 60min of international call within a bill period.
    • Tiered or percentage based discounts on one or more bill line items based on aggregated charges that apply to a particular bill cycle or financial terms.
    Fee A fixed Charge calculated for a service performed by a person/system or as a compensation to cover the cost of a process whether be human-centric or system-controlled, such as; consulting fee, late payment fee etc.
    Monetization Monetization refers to standards and processes defined/adopted for deriving revenue from the value you offer to your customers.
    Price (a.k.a List Price) The amount of money expected to be paid or set as consideration (typically on a Tariff/Price Plan) for a sales item; services rendered or hard/soft goods supplied.
    Price Plan (a.k.a Tariff) Please refer to definition for Tariff
    Promotion An integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used to increase sales, product public awareness. Promotions shall not be confused with Discounts. A promotion may use one or more several tools to increase sales and awareness, such as; loyalty points, bonus points, advertising and discounting.
    Rate Rate can be considered as a specialization of Price. Thus, Price may always be used interchangeably with Rate. Rate is a defined Price value of a sales item for a specific context according to a ratio, scale, or standard, such as; 0.05$ per minute of domestic phone call, 1$ per minute of international phone call.
    Tariff (a.k.a Price Plan) An integrated context of schedule and/or scheme and/or formulas used for calculation/aggregation purposes by Charging and Billing processes. Please refer to definitions for: Charging Tariff, Billing Tariff
